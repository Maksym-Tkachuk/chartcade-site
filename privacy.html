Privacy Policy for GlobalPace

Last updated: April 28, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the GlobalPace application ("GlobalPace", "we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use GlobalPace to render and publish chart-animation videos. GlobalPace is hosted at https://maksym-tkachuk.github.io/chartcade-site/.

2. Information We Collect

GlobalPace collects only the minimum information necessary to operate the app:

  • Authentication tokens: OAuth tokens from YouTube and TikTok are stored locally on your device so GlobalPace can publish videos on your behalf. These tokens are never transmitted to any server operated by GlobalPace.
  • Video content: Videos rendered by GlobalPace are processed locally on your device and uploaded directly from your device to your YouTube and TikTok accounts.

3. Information GlobalPace Does NOT Collect

  • GlobalPace does not collect personal identification information
  • GlobalPace does not collect email addresses
  • GlobalPace does not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • GlobalPace does not collect analytics or usage data
  • GlobalPace does not store your videos on any server

4. How GlobalPace Uses Information

Authentication tokens are used solely so GlobalPace can publish content to your connected YouTube and TikTok accounts on your behalf. No data collected by GlobalPace is used for advertising, profiling, resale, or any purpose other than the publishing actions you initiate.

5. Data Storage

All data used by GlobalPace, including OAuth tokens and application settings, is stored locally on your device. GlobalPace does not operate servers that store user data. Tokens live in your local configuration directory and can be deleted by you at any time.

6. Third-Party Services

GlobalPace connects to the following third-party APIs to deliver its functionality:

  • YouTube Data API v3 — for uploading videos and posting comments from GlobalPace to YouTube. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • TikTok Content Posting API — for publishing videos from GlobalPace to TikTok. Subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy.

7. Data Sharing

GlobalPace does not sell, trade, or transfer your information to third parties. Data is only transmitted to the YouTube and TikTok APIs as required to fulfill the publishing requests you initiate from within GlobalPace.

8. Data Retention

Authentication tokens remain on your device until you delete them. GlobalPace does not retain any copies of your data on remote servers.

9. Your Rights

While using GlobalPace you have the right to:

  • Delete your stored authentication tokens at any time
  • Revoke GlobalPace's access through your YouTube or TikTok account settings
  • Request information about what data GlobalPace accesses

10. Children's Privacy

GlobalPace is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. GlobalPace does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy for GlobalPace may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or about GlobalPace, please contact us at datapulse.channel@gmail.com.
