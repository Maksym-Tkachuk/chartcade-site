Privacy Policy for Chartcade

Last updated: April 28, 2026

← Back to Chartcade

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Chartcade application ("Chartcade", "we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use Chartcade to render and publish chart-animation videos. Chartcade is hosted at https://maksym-tkachuk.github.io/chartcade-site/.

2. Information We Collect

Chartcade collects only the minimum information necessary to operate the app:

  • Authentication tokens: OAuth tokens from YouTube and TikTok are stored locally on your device so Chartcade can publish videos on your behalf. These tokens are never transmitted to any server operated by Chartcade.
  • Video content: Videos rendered by Chartcade are processed locally on your device and uploaded directly from your device to your YouTube and TikTok accounts.

3. Information Chartcade Does NOT Collect

  • Chartcade does not collect personal identification information
  • Chartcade does not collect email addresses
  • Chartcade does not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Chartcade does not collect analytics or usage data
  • Chartcade does not store your videos on any server

4. How Chartcade Uses Information

Authentication tokens are used solely so Chartcade can publish content to your connected YouTube and TikTok accounts on your behalf. No data collected by Chartcade is used for advertising, profiling, resale, or any purpose other than the publishing actions you initiate.

5. Data Storage

All data used by Chartcade, including OAuth tokens and application settings, is stored locally on your device. Chartcade does not operate servers that store user data. Tokens live in your local configuration directory and can be deleted by you at any time.

6. Third-Party Services

Chartcade connects to the following third-party APIs to deliver its functionality:

  • YouTube Data API v3 — for uploading videos and posting comments from Chartcade to YouTube. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • TikTok Content Posting API — for publishing videos from Chartcade to TikTok. Subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy.

7. Data Sharing

Chartcade does not sell, trade, or transfer your information to third parties. Data is only transmitted to the YouTube and TikTok APIs as required to fulfill the publishing requests you initiate from within Chartcade.

8. Data Retention

Authentication tokens remain on your device until you delete them. Chartcade does not retain any copies of your data on remote servers.

9. Your Rights

While using Chartcade you have the right to:

  • Delete your stored authentication tokens at any time
  • Revoke Chartcade's access through your YouTube or TikTok account settings
  • Request information about what data Chartcade accesses

10. Children's Privacy

Chartcade is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. Chartcade does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy for Chartcade may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or about Chartcade, please contact us at datapulse.channel@gmail.com.
