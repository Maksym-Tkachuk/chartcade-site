GlobalPace

GlobalPace is a desktop tool that turns historical datasets into animated bar-race, countdown, and world-map chart videos and publishes them to YouTube Shorts and TikTok.

Render

Generate vertical 1080x1920 chart animations locally from curated datasets.

Publish

Upload directly to your own YouTube and TikTok accounts via official APIs.

Private by design

OAuth tokens stay on your device. No analytics, no servers, no third-party tracking.
